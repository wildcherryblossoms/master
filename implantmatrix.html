IMPLANT MATRIX


INTERACCESS GALLERY / Toronto
Miriam Ho in collaboration with Philip Beesley Architect

The Site Magazine is Canada’s longest running independent architecture magazine. For over fifteen years, we have probed a broad and incisive spectrum of topics in art and architecture, urbanism and landscape, attracting contributors and readers from around the world. The Site Magazine continues this tradition of publishing profound and provocative thinking with a new modern visual identity, an expanded editorial team, and new strategies for print and digital distribution. The Site Magazine is largely written by, and for, emerging designers and thinkers. We publish original writing, design and art from new voices with fresh ideas. Every issue is content-driven, and carefully curated to share the nuances of architectural thinking with the general public. By bringing a Canadian lens to pressing global issues, we aim to cultivate a community of creative and critical thinkers who can influence design futures in Canada and abroad.

The Site Magazine is the winner of Best Magazine and Best Arts and Literary at the National Magazine Awards 2018.
