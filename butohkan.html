GHOSTS OF THE ARAL SEA


SHORT FILM / ONGOING
Lucas Smith in collaboration with Kyoto Butoh-Kan

This short documentary is an ongoing project borne out of Lucas's passion for depicting dance and motion by finding a way to choreograph the camera to emphasize the character of the performance. This section features Yurabe Masami's solo dance, "Underworld Flower" performed at Kyoto Butoh-Kan in Feburary 2017.

Butoh-Kan represents Lucas and Miriam's third ongoing collaboration.
