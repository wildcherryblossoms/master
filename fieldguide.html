FIELD GUIDE


INSTALLATION / Gladstone Hotel, 2013
Miriam Ho in collaboration with F_RMLAB

Field Guide is an interactive sculpture and spatial installation composed of an intricate modular surface that is manipulated by an embedded digital infrastructure. Programmed to respond to the motion of passing viewers, the installation provokes our participation and dynamic engagement with everyday spaces. of site topography to create a sensous playground that ties the buildings into their forest setting..

Field Guide was selected for the curated annual show "Grow-Op" at Gladstone Hotel, Toronto in 2013.
